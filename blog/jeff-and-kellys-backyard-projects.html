Jeff and Kelly's Backyard Projects

Sun 20 March 2016
By Jeff Meadows

Here's a look at some of the things we've been working on in our yard over the last few weeks.

Elevated Planter Beds

Kelly and I wanted to start gardening, but our back yard is fully hardscaped, so there's nowhere to plant!

We decided to build some gardening beds that are elevated so we can garden without bending down.

Here are some pictures of us putting them together. They're made almost entirely out of 2x4s.

Here's the finished products.

Salad Table

We've also started gardening in plastic tubs. They're great because they're small and easy to move. We decided to build a table to hold 3 of them.

First, I build a frame out of 2x4s and 2x2s.

Next, we build a stand on wheels.

Here are the plants in the frame.

Deck

Our yard is split in two by a creek. We built a deck in what we call our "second back yard."

Brick Planter

The second back yard has some chain link fence to keep us from falling into the creek. We built a long brick planter for grapevines to crawl up the fence.

Bread

OK, this wasn't baked in the back yard. But it was delicious - fig raisin walnut wheat bread!
